Donate your own AI. Pick a promising planet, copy the ready-made research prompt, run it in your own ChatGPT, Claude, or Gemini, and paste the answer back. You're helping build cited, structured "dossiers" on the best candidate worlds — work that scales far past what humans can read alone. We never use your account or your tokens.

How this stays honest: your dossier is one input, not the final word. In the live version every target is researched by several people using different AIs, and we only trust facts they agree on. We also mix in known-answer targets to score how reliable each contributor's AI pipeline is, and every fact must carry a source we can check. AI proposes; humans and the professional record dispose.
