Answers shared here are pooled across every explorer. Add yours, then validate others' — only what the community trusts advances toward the professional record.

How this stays honest: a single AI's answer is never the final word. The Lab only marks a fact corroborated when independent AIs agree; anything just one AI says stays single-source, and disagreements are flagged as conflicts for a human. Every fact must carry a source we can check, and in the live project we also mix in known-answer targets to score each AI pipeline. AI proposes; humans and the professional record dispose.
